Who are we?

As first-year Drexel students, we are anxious and curious about the co-op process, and our inquries sparked the idea of Dragon-Op. When beginning this project, we were completely unaware that there is a similar resource, ES&P Archives, so this could be considered an homage, or a revamping, in order to make more Drexel students aware of the experiences of fellow Dragons. As we, and Mario the Magnificent, preach honesty, we hope to let all of users get to know a bit about who we are!
