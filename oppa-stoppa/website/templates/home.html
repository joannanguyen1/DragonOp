What is Dragon-Op?

Drexel students cannot blindly apply to co-ops. It’s true that Drexel-affiliated co-ops have plenty of information and resources for students to research before applying. However, regardless of how professional companies may seem on the outside, there’s no better information to have than first-hand experiences. Dragon-Op is a forum website that allows Drexel students to submit reviews and ratings on co-ops they’ve been through, describing their experiences for those wondering which co-ops are worth working at.

Our mission

We aim to provide every Drexel student with real and honest opinions and reviews of Drexel-affliated co-ops from fellow dragons!

Why?

As first-year Drexel students, we wanted to create a discussion platform for other students who are unsure or nervous about their co-op. We feel like there is little known resources to aid the co-op process other than your advisor, however wouldn't you like to hear from those who have actually participated in the co-op?
